Hello! I'm Dylan.

I created DTransit in February 2023. I have been hugely passionate about trains and planes since I was a little kid. Growing up in the New York City metro area meant very regular trips to the Big Apple, always using some form of public transit. I decided to following my passions, and started to make research-based videos about trains and planes, to an audience of roughly 620 subscibers for a total of 59 thousand views, as of July 2023.
